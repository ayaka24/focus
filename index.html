About

The procedure is very simple. All you have to do is bring the product or send it to us. Any genre is welcome. The YANBARU HOSTEL creative team will take photos and edit based on the contents of the preliminary interview survey, and will deliver them as promotional materials that can be used for a long time within a minimum of one week. After checking, if there are no defects, you will receive payment and the product will be returned. Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions. With sophisticated spaces and reliable technology, we help companies and individuals to highlight their uniqueness, improve their brand value, and develop sales channels.

FOCUS OPERATION SYSTEM

  • 2.Receipt of goods (Bring or mail)
